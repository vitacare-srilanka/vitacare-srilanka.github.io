How it works

The accountability loop

VitaCare doesn't stop at a reminder. It closes the loop with a real person — and a timestamp.

Step 1

Patient takes the dose

A clear reminder by notification, alarm or email. Large buttons and colour-coded schedules made for elderly users.

Step 2

Caregiver confirms it

A real caregiver verifies the dose was taken. Each confirmation is logged with a timestamp — proof, not a guess.

Step 3

Family is alerted

If no one confirms in time, the alert escalates to backup caregivers and family — so a missed dose never goes unnoticed.

Every confirmation becomes a timestamped medication history a doctor can review between visits.
